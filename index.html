About Us

Lakeview Swim Club (LVSC) is a non-profit, professionally managed community pool located in the Kings Park West neighborhood of Fairfax County. The pool is situated on a beautifully-wooded lot at the shore of Royal Lake, with a short walk to tennis and basketball courts and a playground at Royal Lake Park. Our Highlights


  • Professionally managed, assuring quality, and less closures.
  • Home of Marlins Swim & Dive team, plus Mighty Marlins junior and off-season programs.
  • Youth lessons and Masters swim training run by Cardinal Aquatics.
  • Social events through the season (Food Trucks, Raft Nights, 4th of July Celebration, Father’s Day Celebration, Parties, Dining Outs & More).
  • Kids’ slide, two diving boards, lap lane, and enclosed toddler pool.
  • Large parking lot, allowing safe and easy arrivals.
  • Opportunities for birthday party or social event hosting.
  • Proactive and engaged Board of Directors.


Reciprocity


Lakeview has a reciprocal agreement across Lakeview, Commonwealth, Kings Ridge, and Sideburn Run Swim Clubs.

Members of each pool may use neighboring pools when their pool is closed due to swim meets, operational, or mechanical issues. These agreements exclude Independence and Labor Day holidays.


In order to take advantage of this agreement, please:


  1. Click here to print a form
  2. Bring it for signature by the Lakeview Guard Manager/Asst. Manager on-duty.
  3. You can then bring this form and visit our partner facilities.
